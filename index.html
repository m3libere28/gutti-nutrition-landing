About Me

Hi, I'm Emily.

RDN, LDN | Community Educator | Patient-Centered Care

Supporting families at every stage. As a Registered Dietitian, I am dedicated to empowering families through nutrition education and compassionate support.

I specialize in Pediatric, Maternal, and Family Systems Nutrition—providing patient-centered care that honors your unique needs and cultural traditions. My approach is rooted in the rigorous pedagogy of dietetics: Assessment, Diagnosis, Intervention, and Monitoring, but delivered with a focus on practical, sustainable change for the whole family.
