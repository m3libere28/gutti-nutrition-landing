5 Signs You Have SIBO (And What To Do About It)

Emily Torres-Medaglia, RDN
December 15, 2025 • 5 min read

Bloating isn't just "normal." If you look 6 months pregnant after eating a salad, something else is going on.

What is SIBO?

Small Intestinal Bacterial Overgrowth (SIBO) occurs when bacteria that normally live in the large intestine migrate upwards...

Common Symptoms

  • Bloating immediately after meals
  • Abdominal pain and cramping
  • Constipation, diarrhea, or both
  • Brain fog and fatigue

Finding the Root Cause

Treating SIBO isn't just about killing the bacteria; it's about finding out why they are there in the first place.
